## Body

**Header:** Why we don't have a price list (yet)

> Mein Scope is early. We'd rather talk to a handful of clinics directly and get the product right than publish a price list before we've earned it. If you're evaluating Mein Scope, tell us a bit about your clinic (number of clinicians, procedure volume, single site or multiple) and we'll follow up with pricing.

---

## What to expect

- A short conversation about how your clinic currently handles intake, capture, and reporting
- A live walkthrough of Mein Scope on a real or test device
- A pricing quote based on your setup, no obligation

---

## CTA

**CTA:** `Talk to Us About Pricing`

*Annotation:* Deliberately avoids tiers, "starting at $X," or any self-serve language. The brief is explicit that billing/accounts don't exist yet. Framing the absence of a price list as a product-stage fact, not a hidden-cost dodge, keeps it honest rather than evasive.
